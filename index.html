Megha Modhak

A dynamic individual, seamlessly weaves her pursuits as a philosophy student and artist. Currently pursuing her MA, Megha's inquisitive mind dives deep into existential inquiries, ethics, and epistemology. Drawing from philosophical inspiration, she skillfully crafts breathtaking artwork that speaks volumes about the human experience. Through painting, sketching, and sculpting, Megha's creations evoke introspection and connection. Embracing the interplay of academia and creativity, she finds harmony between critical thinking and imaginative expression. Megha's journey as a philosopher-artist reflects her passion for profound understanding and her ability to communicate emotions and ideas, leaving an indelible impression on the world.
